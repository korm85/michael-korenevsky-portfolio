## AMVero — Product & Pricing

- **[Alerts Feature PRD](https://docs.google.com/document/d/1zoWRqvujh96Wlu9V3eaDodg8C0gYmsjvuJvhB0HCPKo/edit)** — High-level product requirements document for the real-time alerting feature, covering user scenarios, decision logic, and acceptance criteria.

- **[AMVero Licensing ROI Optimizer](https://drive.google.com/file/d/1GbkdsS5vJLpCvJQOYmCaMfCiRhDfAl6T/view)** — Interactive simulation tool built to model and validate the credit-based licensing model. Takes fleet size, print volume, and layer resolution as inputs; outputs cost comparison and break-even threshold against the legacy per-machine license. Used internally to define optimal credit pricing and by sales teams in customer conversations.
